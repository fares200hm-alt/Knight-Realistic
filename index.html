PPFalter Filter

The Filter

Knight Realistic Pro is a complete post-processing filter for Assetto Corsa built on the Pure platform. A fully dynamic lighting system that reacts to time of day, weather conditions, and your camera position.

  • Dynamic tunnel exposure with flash effect on exit
  • Sun & Moon dual godray system — 4 modes each
  • 5 advanced bloom presets (Star, Natural, Custom)
  • Adaptive tonemapping (ACES Filmic / Unreal Engine)
  • 9 color grading presets (Autumn, Summer, Night Crawler…)
  • World lighting LUT — 12-point sun elevation table

Filter v1.4 — Complete System Overhaul

The most powerful filter yet — v1.4 surpasses both v1.2 and v1.3 in every way. A completely rebuilt world lighting system, new Tunnel Exposure LUT with flash effect, and a dual Sun+Moon godray system that makes previous versions look outdated.

  • NEWTunnel Exposure System — 9-point LUT with flash on exit
  • NEWWorld Lighting LUT — 12-point sun elevation table
  • NEWDual Godray System — Sun & Moon simultaneously
  • NEW5 Bloom presets including Star 8-point and Custom
  • ADD3 new LUT presets: Autumn, Summer, Metropolitan Pure
  • UPDShader pipeline optimized — avg +8 FPS on mid-range GPUs
  • FIXResolved flicker at ultrawide resolutions
SkinSRP v1.4 — New Surface System

SkinSRP now ships with a completely new surface material system.

  • NEWRed Trees Edition — deep crimson tree canopy
  • NEWNew surface material system with improved PBR
  • UPDAll previous variants re-exported at higher quality
  • FIXFixed texture seam on coastal terrain sections
Knight Tool v1 — All-In-One AC App

Brand new exclusive app for Assetto Corsa. Three powerful modules in one package.

  • NEWTrack Skin Changer with grid UI & live preview
  • NEWUnderglow System — 12 colors, 8 animation modes
  • NEWFlame Tuner — 6 presets, 10 colors, live preview
  • ADDPolice mode (Red/Blue alternating underglow)
